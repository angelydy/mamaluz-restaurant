About

Luz is a housewife who loves to cook and try out new things. Then years later, we've decided to develop the MamaLuz app which contains the daily menu, diets and cooking tips, as well as Q&A's and Latest Videos. Everything you can find in your house: recipes and shopping lists, diet viewer so that you can count calories, and articles where you can find household tips or new ideas for recipes. Bring home the taste and quality you want! We only use the best ingredient to make sure our product arrives fresh to your home every day.

Mission

We are a family-owned business that seeks to bring you the best of what nature has to offer. Our products are made in the purest form, using the most natural ingredients, with minimal processing and zero artificial ingredients. Our mission is to bring quality foods without breaking your pocket.

Vision

We believe that access to food should not be a luxury that is reserved for the few, but rather a fundamental right of every citizen. And we are passionate about changing the way restaurants work. This means offering an innovative dining experience, operating as a sustainable business and creating an opportunity for everyone to connect with food and each other in a new way.
